The Uzumaki Clan (うずまき一族, Uzumaki Ichizoku) is formerly a prominent clan in Uzushiogakure. Since its disbandment, most of its known members reside in Konohagakure, with the Fifth and Seventh Hokage being descendants of the clan.[1] Despite its demise, fifteen years after the Fourth Shinobi World War the clan is once again active
