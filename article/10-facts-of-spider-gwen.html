( 2 ) Peter parker's death

The bullying Peter suffered worsens, and eventually he creates a formula that turns him into a lizard mutantOn prom night, after he is being bullied yet again, he injects himself with the formula and transforms, going crazy. Peter regresses to his human form in Gwen's arms, telling her he just wanted to "be special" like her and prompting her to realize what she has done
